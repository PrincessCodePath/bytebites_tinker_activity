## Client Feature Request

The ByteBites app needs backend logic to manage customers and food orders. The system should store customer information, including their name and past purchases, so the app can recognize returning users.

Customers should be able to browse food items like burgers or drinks. Each item should store its name, price, category, and popularity rating. The app also needs a menu that keeps track of all available items and allows users to filter them by category such as drinks or desserts.

When a customer selects items, they should be grouped into an order. The order should store the selected items and calculate the total price.

## Candidate Classes

1. Customer
2. Menu Item
3. Menu
4. Order
